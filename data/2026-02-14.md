### [1] [Maximizing Index Diversity in Committee Elections](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11400)
*Paula Böhm,Robert Bredereck,Till Fluschnik*

Main category: cs.GT

TL;DR: 提出两种考虑候选人标签的多数胜选举模型，旨在平衡委员会得分与多样性，引入生态学多样性指标并分析计算复杂度


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的多数胜选举模型主要关注委员会得分（如投票规则得分），但忽略了委员会多样性的重要性。需要开发能够同时考虑得分和多样性的模型，特别是在候选人带有标签（如性别、种族、专业背景）的情况下。

Method: 1) 提出两种模型：模型一在给定得分下限下最大化多样性；模型二在满足每个代理最低满意度下最大化多样性。2) 使用生态学多样性指标并引入新指标。3) 定义多样性指标的性质并测试。4) 分析计算复杂度并进行实证研究。

Result: 1) 定义了多样性指标的期望性质并测试了现有指标。2) 新提出的多样性指标具有良好性质并被特征化。3) 分析了两种模型在不同投票规则下的计算复杂度。4) 实证研究表明放宽得分或满意度约束可以显著提高多样性。

Conclusion: 该研究为多数胜选举提供了考虑多样性的框架，展示了如何在得分约束下优化多样性，为实际选举系统设计提供了理论依据和实用工具。

Abstract: We introduce two models of multiwinner elections with approval preferences and labelled candidates that take the committee's diversity into account. One model aims to find a committee with maximal diversity given a scoring function (e.g. of a scoring-based voting rule) and a lower bound for the score to be respected. The second model seeks to maximize the diversity given a minimal satisfaction for each agent to be respected. To measure the diversity of a committee, we use multiple diversity indices used in ecology and introduce one new index. We define (desirable) properties of diversity indices, test the indices considered against these properties, and characterize the new index. We analyze the computational complexity of computing a committee for both models and scoring functions of well-known voting rules, and investigate the influence of weakening the score or satisfaction constraints on the diversity empirically.

### [2] [Generative AI-Driven Phase Control for RIS-Aided Cell-Free Massive MIMO Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11226)
*Kalpesh K. Patel,Malay Chakraborty,Ekant Sharma,Sandeep Kumar Singh*

Main category: cs.IT

TL;DR: 提出两种基于生成式AI的方法(GCDM和GCDIM)来优化RIS辅助的无蜂窝大规模MIMO系统中的相位偏移，在非理想信道状态信息和空间相关性约束下最大化系统和速率，相比传统专家算法大幅降低计算开销。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在RIS辅助的无蜂窝大规模MIMO系统中，相位偏移优化面临计算复杂度高的问题，特别是在非理想信道状态信息和空间相关性等实际约束下。传统专家算法虽然能达到接近最优解，但计算开销巨大，需要更高效的优化方法。

Method: 提出两种基于生成式AI的方法：生成条件扩散模型(GCDM)和生成条件隐式扩散模型(GCDIM)。这些方法利用以动态信道状态信息为条件的扩散模型来优化RIS相位偏移，最大化系统和速率。

Result: GCDM在系统和速率方面与专家算法相当，但显著降低了计算开销。GCDIM实现了可比的系统和速率，同时计算时间进一步减少了98%，展示了在RIS辅助无蜂窝大规模MIMO系统中高效相位优化的潜力。

Conclusion: 基于生成式AI的方法为RIS相位偏移优化提供了高效解决方案，GCDIM特别适合实时应用，在保持性能的同时大幅降低计算复杂度，为RIS辅助通信系统的实际部署提供了有前景的技术路径。

Abstract: This work investigates a generative artificial intelligence (GenAI) model to optimize the reconfigurable intelligent surface (RIS) phase shifts in RIS-aided cell-free massive multiple-input multiple-output (mMIMO) systems under practical constraints, including imperfect channel state information (CSI) and spatial correlation. We propose two GenAI based approaches, generative conditional diffusion model (GCDM) and generative conditional diffusion implicit model (GCDIM), leveraging the diffusion model conditioned on dynamic CSI to maximize the sum spectral efficiency (SE) of the system. To benchmark performance, we compare the proposed GenAI based approaches against an expert algorithm, traditionally known for achieving near-optimal solutions at the cost of computational efficiency. The simulation results demonstrate that GCDM matches the sum SE achieved by the expert algorithm while significantly reducing the computational overhead. Furthermore, GCDIM achieves a comparable sum SE with an additional $98\%$ reduction in computation time, underscoring its potential for efficient phase optimization in RIS-aided cell-free mMIMO systems.
