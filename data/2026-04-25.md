### [1] [On Time-Memory Tradeoffs for Maximal Palindromes with Wildcards and $k$-Mismatches](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21140)
*Amihood Amir,Ayelet Butman,Michael Itzhaki,Dina Sokol*

Main category: cs.DS

TL;DR: 本文研究了含通配符文本中的回文因子识别问题，利用通配符-LCE技术实现了连续时间-内存权衡，提出了首个非平凡线性空间算法来计算所有带通配符的最大回文，并在某些参数范围内改进了已知的最佳时间-内存乘积。主要成果包括查找和近似所有最大回文的算法，并将两种方法推广到k- mismatches设置（含或不含通配符）。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 通配符（可匹配任意字符的特殊符号）的存在破坏了经典字符串算法的许多组合性质，使得现有回文识别算法无法直接应用。因此需要专门针对含通配符文本设计高效算法。

Method: 采用通配符-LCE（最长公共扩展）技术实现连续的时间-内存权衡；提出首个非平凡线性空间算法；将算法推广至k- mismatches（k次不匹配容忍）场景。

Result: 在某些参数范围内改进了已知的最佳时间-内存乘积；提出了首个非平凡线性空间算法；方法成功推广至k- mismatches设置。

Conclusion: 该研究为含通配符文本中的回文识别问题提供了更高效的解决方案，并通过推广到k- mismatches设置拓展了应用范围。

Abstract: This paper addresses the problem of identifying palindromic factors in texts that include wildcards -- special characters that match all others. These symbols challenge many classical algorithms, as numerous combinatorial properties are not satisfied in their presence. We apply existing wildcard-LCE techniques to obtain a continuous time-memory tradeoff, and present the first non-trivial linear-space algorithm for computing all maximal palindromes with wildcards, improving the best known time-memory product in certain parameter ranges. Our main results are algorithms to find and approximate all maximal palindromes in a given text. We also generalize both methods to the $k$-mismatches setting, with or without wildcards.
